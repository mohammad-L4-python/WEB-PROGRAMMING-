Plot Overview

Snowfall depicts the early days of the crack cocaine epidemic in Los Angeles during the 1980s.The series follows multiple characters, including Franklin Saint, a young street entrepreneur, as they navigate the dangerous world of drug trafficking.

Cast

  • Damson Idris as Franklin Saint
  • Carter Hudson as Teddy McDonald
  • Emily Rios as Lucia Villanueva
  • Sergio Peris-Mencheta as Gustavo "El Oso" Zapata
  • Isaiah John as Leon Simmons
