Afghani Biryani

Afghani Biryani is a flavorful rice dish with tender pieces of meat and aromatic spices. This traditional Afghan recipe is perfect for special occasions and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 500g lamb or chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro or mint leaves for garnish

Step-By-Step

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  4. Add the meat pieces to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Stir in the yogurt and spices (turmeric, coriander, cumin, garam masala, chili powder, and salt). Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Stir well to combine with the meat and spices.
  7. Pour enough water into the pot to cover the rice by about an inch. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh cilantro or mint leaves.
  9. Serve hot with yogurt or your favorite chutney on the side. Enjoy your delicious Afghani Biryani!
